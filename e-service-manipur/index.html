E-Services Manipur

The Government of Manipur has launched e-Services Manipur, a unified platform to streamline access to various government services. This blog provides an easy-to-follow guide on what e-Services Manipur is, how to use it, and the essential features you need to know.

What is e-Services Manipur?

e-Services Manipur is an online portal developed by the Government of Manipur to simplify the delivery of government services to citizens. It includes services such as:

  • Applying for certificates (e.g., OBC, Domicile, Permanent Resident, SC/ST certificates, Death, Birth)
  • Click here to checking the status of applications.
  • Click here to download your certificates.
  • Accessing other government-related services.

This portal aims to save time, reduce paperwork, and provide transparency in service delivery.

How to Apply OBC, Domicile, Permanent Resident, SC/ST certificates, Death, Birth etc

To apply online, you need to register first. Here are the steps to Register on e-Pramaan:

  1. Access the e-Services Manipur Portal:

    Open a web browser and navigate to https://eservicesmanipur.gov.in.

  2. Initiate Registration:

    Click on the "Click here to Login with ePramaan" link. If you're a new user, select the "Register" option.

  3. Choose Registration Method:

    Opt for "Registration using Aadhaar Number."

  4. Enter Aadhaar Details:
    • Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
    • Select the OTP delivery method: either mobile or email.
    • Agree to the terms by checking the appropriate box.
    • Complete the CAPTCHA verification.
    • Click on "Verify through e-KYC."
  5. OTP Verification:
    • An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number or email associated with Aadhaar.
    • Enter the received OTP in the designated field.
    • Use the "Regenerate OTP for Aadhaar e-KYC" option if needed (up to four attempts).
  6. Complete Registration Form:
    • Details fetched from Aadhaar, such as Name, DOB, and Aadhaar Number, will be pre-filled.
    • Fill in any additional information if required.
  7. Submit Registration:
    • After completing the form, submit it to create your e-Pramaan account.
    • Verify your email via the link and your mobile number using the code sent to you.

Now your account is ready to use.

Documents required

  1. Voter ID of applicant or of parents
  2. Any identity proof of the applicant e.g., Aadhaar
  3. Up-to-date Jamabandi or Patta
  4. Up-to-date pay slip of Jamabandi ( Khajana)
  5. Passport size Photograph
  6. Other required Document 1
  7. Other required Document 2
  8. Other required Document 3
(Only files up to 250 KB are accepted)

How to Log In to e-Services Manipur

  1. Visit the official website: Go to e-Services Manipur.
  2. Click on "Login": You’ll find the login button on the homepage.
  3. Enter your credentials: Input your registered email or mobile number and password.
  4. Click on "Sign In": You will be redirected to your dashboard.

How to Apply for Services

  1. Log in to your account: Access the dashboard.
  2. Choose a service: From the menu, select the service you want to apply for (e.g., Birth Certificate).
  3. Fill in the application form: Provide the required details and upload necessary documents.
  4. Submit the application: Verify the information and submit your form.
  5. Pay the fee (if applicable): Not applicable in this case.

How to Check Application Status

  1. Go to E Service Manipur Home Page
  2. Navigate to "Application Status": This option is available on the dashboard.
  3. Enter your application ID: You’ll receive this ID after submitting your form.
  4. View the status: The system will display the current status of your application.

How to Download Issued Certificates

  1. Go to E Service Manipur Home Page
  2. Go to the "Verify / Print Certificate" section: Available on the dashboard.
  3. Enter your application ID: You’ll receive this ID after submitting your form.
  4. Download the file: Click on the "Download" button to save the certificate.

How to e-Sign Documents

  1. Open the Document in Adobe Acrobat
  2. Select "E-Sign" Tool
  3. Validate Signature
  4. Print the Document
